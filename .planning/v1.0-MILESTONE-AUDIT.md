**What:** Phase 6 added study links to `DayTimeline.tsx`, but that component was superseded by `CalendarView`'s `DayView`. The study links exist in dead code.

**Fix:** Add study link logic to CalendarView's DayView, mirroring DayTimeline lines 145-151.

### 2. Upload Page Navigation (VIEW-01 partial)

**What:** `app/syllabi/upload/page.tsx` uses a custom `<header>` instead of the shared `NavHeader` component. Users can't navigate to dashboard, plan, or other pages from the upload screen.

**Fix:** Replace custom header with `<NavHeader />`.
